About

I attend the University of Mary Washington. I am studying Biology and Business Administration. I currently work in the business marketing field with interests in breaking into hospital administration/management. At my previous position at the Freedom Aquatics & Fitness Center, I received training in First Aid/CPR/AED and Medical Administrative Training (MAT). I am a member of the UMW RFC, Mother's Rugby. We achieved an undefeated season and became DIAA National Champions in our 2017-2018 season. I currently live in Fredericksburg, VA with my dog. In my free time I like to enjoy playing games with friends, read, and go to the gym.
